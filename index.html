Knowledge of the Day
Why does the U.S. use the Electoral College?
Civics ›

美国总统选举不是全国直选,而是通过"选举人团"制度。全国共538张选举人票,候选人需赢得270张以上才能当选。这一制度设计于建国初期,目的是平衡大州与小州的权力——但也导致过"赢得总票数却输掉选举"的情况,如2000年的戈尔和2016年的希拉里。

Quick Quiz
Which sport uses the term "Grand Slam" to mean winning all four major tournaments in one year?
Correct! In tennis, the four Grand Slams are the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and US Open. Winning all four in one year is extremely rare. Note: "Grand Slam" in baseball means something completely different — a home run with bases loaded!
